About Us!

I feel overwhelming pleasure in sharing thoughts about my organisation with you.

"YOU DID" (Youth Undertaking Developed India Dream) is a legally registered Non-Government Organization(NGO) founded in 2018 located at Jayanagar 1st block, Bangalore 560011. It is lead by feminine strength and consists of over 30 members working for it.

YOU DID team has highly disciplined and well-qualified members pursuing their degrees from reputed academic institutions.

The organization aims at the betterment of society by allowing people to lead a happy and healthier life. To create awareness in education, augmenting government policies, cleaning the city, planting saplings, educating people about cleanliness, visiting hospitals, feeding the hungry, supporting flood relief, providing health tips, visiting old-age homes and orphanages etc. We have future plans to set up branches in rural areas and starting camps, building community halls, bus stands, basic facilities etc., and continue our ongoing engagement. It aims to bring a positive change in people and society.

As an organization, we strive to do good at all levels and give our best while at it. We inspire people to become aware of the essence of a positive life with good-deeds and faith in the possibility of change.

Our volunteers actively participate in all engagements. They take time out and contribute to society in what we believe. We have conducted numerous camps in various sectors and continue to expand in all possible ways. Volunteers are like-minded people who are interested in helping, continue to stay with us and work diligently to be part of the camps.

When we trained 10th-grade students of government school to crack their board exams, the school increased their passing percentage by 5%. This is one of such several milestones that continue to keep us motivated.
We have organised a quiz " Brain Break" Where we had 1k registrations and participants and raised Rs1 for every right answer from You DID and the raised amount is about 24k for serving the needy in covid-19, distributed around 600 food packets and grocery kit for 35 families.

Currently, we do not have any tie-ups with any fundraisers for funding the organisation. All of our team members contribute voluntarily towards the camps and make the best use of it. We take pride in being completely transparent in all our financial dealings.

We impact people's lives as we believe in giving back a portion of what we have earned, as we believe in helping others by creating opportunities, which is consistent to our tagline "Let The Change Flow".

To see what we do and how we do it, you can always reach out to the Facebook page "You Did", our YouTube channel "You Did" and follow our page on Instagram @youdid.ngo
